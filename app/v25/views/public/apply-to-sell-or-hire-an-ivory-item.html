Use this service to apply to sell or hire elephant ivory items legally.

Published 2 December 2019
From:
Animal and Plant Health Agency

You can be fined £250,000 or sent to prison, or both, if you sell or hire an ivory item that does not have a valid registration or certificate.

You must also be sure that the item is not banned under the Ivory Act.

Apply

You'll need:

  • a photo of the item
  • a debit or credit card

It costs ££ to register or £££ to apply for a certificate.
