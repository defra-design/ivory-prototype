How to buy elephant ivory

Check whether you are legally allowed to buy or hire an ivory item, and whether the item has been registered or certified with us.

Published 1 October 2021
From:
Animal and Plant Health Agency

Overview

If you are buying or hiring an ivory item from someone else, you must be certain that the item is exempt before you proceed.

We can seize items that we later find out were traded illegally. We can also fine or prosecute anyone who buys or hires illegal ivory if we discover that they did not do everything possible to comply with the law.
