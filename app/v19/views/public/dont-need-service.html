Other legal obligations when you sell or hire out the item

As part of the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES), you may need to apply for a CITES article 10 certificate (opens in a new window or tab).

You only need to do this if your item was made between 3 March 1947 and 1 January 1975.

You may also need to apply for a CITES permit (opens in a new window or tab).

You’ll only need to do this if your item will move into or out of Great Britain (England, Scotland and Wales) when you sell or hire it out.
