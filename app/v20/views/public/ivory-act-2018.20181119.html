Exceptions requiring a permit

You must have a permit before you can sell or hire any of the following:

  • Items with less than 10% ivory made before 1947
  • Musical instrument (including bows) with less than 20% ivory and made before 1975
  • Portrait miniatures made before 1918
  • Items to be acquired by an accredited museum
  • An item of outstandingly high artistic, cultural or historical value

There is a charge to use this service.
